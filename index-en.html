International day of the friend 2015
18 JULY 2015 from 9:00 pm to 01:00 am.

On Saturday the 18th July 2015 the” International Day of the Friend” Will be celebrated in Benalmádena with the slogan “Municipality of Peace and Friendship”with the colaboration of Benalmádena (Málaga) Town Council-youth area. It will take place on the beach (Sunset Beach) between the hotels Best Benalmádena and Torrequebrada from 9pm to 1am. TT A picnic of friendship where those attending should wear white, bring towel, and also food and drinks to share. We can enjoy musical shows and perfomances and choreographies of different artistes.
