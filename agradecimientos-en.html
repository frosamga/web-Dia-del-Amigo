We invite you on July 18, 2015!

To my friends…

Málaga, the 5th of December 2014, with excitement I prepare my backpack , in which I place a small notebook with blank pages.

At 9.30 we take off for Barcelona, thousands of people ready to receive the message of Prem Rawat “Ambassador of Peace”.

It’s 4.00pm, and after a warm applause, the event begins. I take a seat, open my notebook and start to write…

“Kindness is a gift we can give one another”

“We don’t accept that some day, nothing will exist”

“To share generosity, gratitude, to know that this breath comes into you, fills you, and allows you Not to turn into dust. And do we celebrate this?”

“To be with yourself, should be a sacred moment with yourself”

“Learn to enjoy. We have no idea what it means to enjoy”

“You have to be in that place where your heart is open, celebrating”

“Focus on celebrating life”

“This life is the Exception”

“We have no idea what it means to be alive”

“We should make our lives exceptional, that’s why we’re here on planet Earth”

Step by step those blank pages were turned into a valuable parchment of wisdom.

I understood that we have the possibility to choose in our lives.

We can leave the pages blank or we can turn them into a valuable parchment.

Carlos
