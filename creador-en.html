Dr. Enrique E. Febbraro- Founder of International day of the friend.

The founder of this festival was the teacher, musician and Argentine dentist Enrique Febbraro, after astronauts Neil Armstrong, Edwin Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins arrived on the moon July 20, 1969. For this Argentine friend, above the epic scientific advance, the landing should also be a way to make new friends in other parts of our planet. "Explained Febbraro- That day, we were all awaiting the fate of the three astronauts. We were his friends and they, friends of the universe."

Old resident of the Argentina city of Lomas de Zamora Buenos -party in recognition of his initiative and by municipal decree, is the Provincial Capital of the Amistad founded there also the World Association for Understanding. He was also professor of psychology at the University of Cossimo Carlo Borromeo (Italy) Foundation, and received an honorary doctorate in social sciences in 1950 by the American Academy of History and Science. Gradually he was working as intended, first it was the Superior Government of the Province of Buenos Aires with his decree No: 235/79 authorized the celebration and gave a legal framework. Then they were adding various entities and extending throughout Argentina and some countries in Latin America.

How can a friend do? Where is it located? The teacher commented that everywhere: in the bar, in the office, another city in the country, just around the corner, through the Internet, traveling, between animals and plants, or in the world the ideas. It just takes a willingness to love, to listen, to understand, to open the other, to give, to feel pleasure to share their joy and to accompany him in his grief.

How do you know the friend? "It's easy, because you can not see, you can feel it," said our friend Febbraro. He also noted that "My friend is my teacher, my pupil and my classmate. He teaches me, I taught him.
