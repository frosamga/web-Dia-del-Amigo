We invite you on July 18, 2015!

To preserve Friendship, from his experience he makes a list to preserve friendship:

The sacraments of friendship are sympathy, tenderness,reciprocity, loyalty, understanding, selflessness, solidarity, perseverance and joy.

My friend is my teacher, my disciple and my mate.

Los sacramentos de la amistad son: simpatía, ternura, respeto, reciprocidad, lealtad, comprensión, desinterés, solidaridad, perseverancia y alegría.

We need to keep silence, even if we are right, because two people can´t fight if one doesn´t want to.

Do not do or say anything to hurt or offend other.

Always be present with someone: in loneliness,pain or joy.

Accept people as they are and love everything about them, without expecting any reward.

Forget your own problems and listen with open ears and open heart to your friends.
