Historia del día del amigo en Benalmádena

History of the "International Day of Friends in Spain: It all started in 2004, through a trip organized by the Department of Youth council of Benalmádena to Barcelona Forum. A group of young people, which we did not know, undertook the adventure of traveling, we had the opportunity to enjoy the art, music and culture from around the world, sharing and met. Without realizing it we discover that the true spirit of the Forum was in us, all that we experienced and shared. Gradually came up in me the need to thank my new Spanish friends friendship and respect that he had received from them. Realizing that the Friend's Day is not celebrated in Spain, I decided that on July 20 of the following year we would meet for this purpose. ... And so on July 20, 2005 was born in Spain our first meeting ... That day was born from the heart, from my respect and gratitude to all those who have opened their hearts and made me feel part of this blessed country, Spain! So we continue each year by meeting waterfront and continued my chance to renew my gratitude. Happy to have had the backing of the creator of International Friendship Day in Argentina, Dr. Don Enrique Ernesto Febbraro, a great man who loved Andalusia, who loved Spain. Happy to have had the support of his wife Doña Olga Pellegrini, spiritual, joyful and happy woman to know that her husband's work began in the old continent. In 2011 it born a new hope, a new challenge, and that the City of Benalmádena institutionalized the "International Day of friends" making Benalmádena and Township Peace and Friendship ", a long sleep we had shared and today is a realidad.¿ Thank you all for making this dream come true! I share these words of my Brazilian friend Rosse: "May your dreams be your banner and Love continue to be your language". I wish that our dreams are our flag and that love continues to be our language " Carlos Alberto Yacovone
